Alternatives to Meta Quest 2

There are a lot of alternative headsets, for example, Pico 4 is another standalone headset created by PicoXR. Valve Index is other example, but it is a non-standalone. The Valve Index, HTC Vive, Lenovo Explorer and other similar headsets need a PC to works and sometimes, they need external sensors.
